Privacy Policy for AI Camera

Last updated: November 2025

AI Camera does not collect personal data from users of the mobile application. The app is intended for authorized school staff only. Login credentials are issued manually by school administrators and are not tied to personal accounts.

The app displays security alerts generated by an external school monitoring system. These alerts may include event metadata (such as time and alert type), but do not contain personal student information within the app interface.

The application does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. The app does not create or store user profiles. No personal identifiers are transmitted to third parties.

Any data processed within the app exists solely for operational school safety purposes and cannot be used to identify individual users or students.

If future updates introduce features such as cloud accounts, usage analytics, or additional data processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated in advance.
